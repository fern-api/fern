## 0.45.0-rc33
**`(feat):`** The Fern CLI now supports roles and viewers in your docs configuration, if you are on the enterprise plan for docs:

```yml docs.ym
roles:
  - internal
  - beta-users
  - enterprise-users

navigation:
  - section: Internal Section
    viewers:
      - internal
    contents:
      - page: Internal Page
        path: ./internal/page.mdx
```
