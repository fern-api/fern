## 0.45.0-rc20
**`(feat):`** The Fern CLI now supports audiences in your docs configuration:

```yml docs.yml
# all audiences must be declared at the top level
audiences:
  - internal

navigation:
  - section: Internal Section
    audience: internal # audience is optional
    contents:
      - page: Internal Page
        path: ./internal/page.mdx
```
