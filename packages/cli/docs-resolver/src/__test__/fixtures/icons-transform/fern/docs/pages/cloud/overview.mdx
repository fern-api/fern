# Welcome to HollowFrame Cloud

HollowFrame Cloud is the next evolution of creative infrastructure — where environments, assets, and code all live in harmony.  
It’s designed for developers, artists, and thinkers who want to deploy ideas as quickly as they dream them up.

Whether you’re spinning up APIs, testing blueprints, or co-editing scenes with a team, HollowFrame Cloud handles the orchestration so you can focus on creation.

## Deploy Instantly

Upload your blueprint, set your environment, and click *Launch.*  
Your app is live before your coffee cools.

## Collaborate Live

Invite teammates into your workspace and build together in real-time. Edits, updates, and commits sync instantly — no waiting, no merging chaos.

## Scale Beautifully

As your project grows, HollowFrame Cloud expands with you.  
From lightweight personal sandboxes to distributed production clusters, scaling is automatic and painless.
